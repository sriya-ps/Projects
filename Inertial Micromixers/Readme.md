* Explored the effect of Reynolds number (Re = 0–300) on mixing efficiency in four inertial micromixer designs using COMSOL Multiphysics.
* Modelled and simulated four designs — ridges, curly baffles, fin-shaped and river-shaped channels.
* Compared the influence of channel geometry on mixing through diffusion, convection, and vortex formation.
* Demonstrated that optimizing micromixer geometry can significantly improve mixing performance for microfluidic and biomedical applications.
